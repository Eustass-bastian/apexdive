Important Information

Essential details to ensure your diving experience is safe and enjoyable

Flying After Diving

For your safety, wait at least 24 hours after your last dive before flying. This reduces the risk of decompression sickness by allowing residual nitrogen to safely exit your body.

Equipment Rental

Full dive gear is available for rent upon request. Our equipment is regularly maintained and serviced to ensure your safety and comfort during all dives.

Non-Diver Options

We offer special rates for non-divers, including guided snorkeling trips, island excursions, and cultural experiences. Everyone can enjoy the beauty of the Maldives.

Weather Conditions

Dive trips may be rescheduled due to adverse weather conditions. Your safety is our priority, and our team will work to ensure you get the most from your diving experience.

All packages include tanks, weights, and professional guides. Personal dive insurance is recommended for all divers.
