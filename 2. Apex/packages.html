Important Information

Essential details about our dive-only packages

Dive-Only Packages

Our packages include only diving activities and equipment. Accommodation and transfers are not included. We're happy to recommend local accommodation options if needed.

Equipment Included

All dive packages include full equipment (BCD, regulator, wetsuit, fins, mask, and weights). Our equipment is regularly maintained and serviced to ensure your safety and comfort.

Scheduling

Dive packages can be completed over consecutive days or spread out during your stay. We'll work with you to create the optimal schedule based on weather conditions and your preferences.

Weather Conditions

Dive trips may be rescheduled due to adverse weather conditions. Your safety is our priority, and our team will work to ensure you get the most from your diving experience.

Personal dive insurance is recommended for all divers. Proof of certification is required for certified divers.
