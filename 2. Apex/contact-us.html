Frequently Asked Questions

  • Yes, basic swimming skills are required for all diving courses. For entry-level courses, you should be able to swim 200 meters without swimming aids and float/tread water for 10 minutes. Safety is our top priority, and being comfortable in water is essential.

  • The minimum age depends on the course level. For Junior Open Water Diver, the minimum age is 10 years. For regular Open Water Diver certification, you must be at least 15 years old. We also offer special "Bubblemaker" experiences for children aged 8-9 years.

  • The Open Water Diver course typically takes 3-4 days to complete. This includes theoretical sessions, confined water training, and open water dives. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your vacation plans.

  • No, all necessary diving equipment is provided for our courses and guided dives. We have high-quality equipment from leading brands in various sizes. If you have your own mask, fins, or snorkel that you're comfortable with, you're welcome to bring them.

  • The Maldives offers excellent diving conditions year-round with water temperatures between 26-30°C (79-86°F). The best visibility (20-30 meters) is generally during the dry season from December to April. The wet season (May to November) offers better chances to see manta rays and whale sharks.
