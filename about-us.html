I'm a professional dive instructor with more than 3 years of experience, and I simply love sharing the magic of the ocean. Discover the breathtaking dive sites that make Rasdhoo Atoll a diver's paradise. From vibrant coral reefs to thrilling drift dives, each location offers unique marine encounters and unforgettable experiences.

Rasdhoo Atoll Dive Sites

Sea Fan Wall

Intermediate

Famous for its colorful sea fans and rich marine biodiversity. Perfect for underwater photography enthusiasts.

Depth: 10-30m
Highlights: Sea fans, reef sharks, schools of snappers

Bodu Gaa

Beginner-Intermediate

A beautiful reef with thriving hard corals and abundant marine life, offering excellent visibility.

Depth: 5-25m
Highlights: Coral formations, reef fish, occasional rays

Three Palms

Beginner

Named after three distinctive coral formations, this site offers diverse marine habitats and encounters.

Depth: 8-25m
Highlights: Unique coral structures, reef fish, turtles

Holhi Faru

Intermediate

A spectacular reef with vibrant coral gardens and diverse marine ecosystems to explore.

Depth: 10-30m
Highlights: Coral gardens, reef sharks, barracudas
5

Reef Explorer Wreck

Intermediate

An artificial reef created by a sunken vessel, now home to diverse marine species and coral growth.

Depth: 15-30m
Highlights: Wreck exploration, marine life aggregations, photography opportunities

Rasdhoo Channel

Advanced

A thrilling drift dive through the channel with chances to spot pelagic species and large predators.

Depth: 12-35m
Highlights: Strong currents, eagle rays, reef sharks, schools of jacks
7

Rasdhoo Beyru

Advanced

An exhilarating wall dive along the outer reef with opportunities to see large pelagic species.

Depth: 15-40m
Highlights: Wall formations, sharks, eagle rays, occasional manta rays
8

Madivaru Channel

Intermediate-Advanced

A dynamic channel dive with varying currents and abundant marine life, including larger predators.

Depth: 10-30m
Highlights: Channel topography, sharks, stingrays, tuna
9

Hammerhead Shark Point

Advanced

The famous early morning dive site with the best chance to encounter schools of hammerhead sharks.

Depth: 25-40m
Highlights: Hammerhead sharks, other pelagic species, wall formations
10

Madivaru Corner

Intermediate-Advanced

A thrilling corner dive with currents that attract a variety of marine life and predators.

Depth: 12-30m
Highlights: Current action, reef sharks, eagle rays, large schools of fish
11

Madivaru Wall

Intermediate

A beautiful wall dive with overhangs, caves, and a rich diversity of marine life to discover.

Depth: 15-35m
Highlights: Wall formations, caves, groupers, turtles, napoleon wrasse
12

Veligandu Sandbank

Beginner

A shallow, scenic dive site near a pristine sandbank, perfect for beginners and photographers.

Depth: 5-18m
Highlights: Clear waters, coral gardens, reef fish, occasional turtles
13

Turtle Reef

Beginner-Intermediate

A shallow reef with high chances of encountering multiple sea turtles and vibrant coral formations.

Depth: 5-20m
Highlights: Green and hawksbill turtles, coral gardens, reef fish
14

Manta Point

Intermediate

A cleaning station frequented by manta rays, offering incredible encounters with these majestic creatures.

Depth: 10-25m
Highlights: Manta rays, cleaning wrasses, reef life, occasional reef sharks
15

North Channel

Advanced

A challenging but rewarding channel dive with potential for encountering large pelagic species.

Depth: 15-40m
Highlights: Strong currents, sharks, large schools of fish, occasional manta rays

The Island

Discover what makes Rasdhoo Island a perfect diving destination

Paradise in the Heart of the Maldives

Rasdhoo is a small but vibrant island located in the North Ari Atoll of the Maldives. As the capital of Alif Alif Atoll, this picturesque destination combines authentic local culture with breathtaking natural beauty, making it an ideal base for divers seeking adventure beneath the waves.

With its pristine white sandy beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and laid-back atmosphere, Rasdhoo offers a perfect balance between diving excitement and peaceful island living. The island spans just 500 meters in diameter, creating an intimate environment where everything is within walking distance.

Strategic Location

Just 56km from Mal, accessible by speedboat (1.5 hours) or domestic flight (20 minutes)

Dive Paradise

Home to 15 world-class dive sites including the famous Hammerhead Point

Perfect Beaches

Enjoy pristine white sand beaches and crystal-clear turquoise waters ideal for relaxation

Local Experience

Experience authentic Maldivian culture while enjoying modern amenities
